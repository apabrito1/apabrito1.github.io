My notes on several Bend-and-Break lemmas.


Let $f:X\to Y$ be a morphism.

$$
H^i(X,\mathcal O_X)=0
$$

$$
\begin{aligned}
K_X &= f^*K_Y + E \\
E^2 &= -1
\end{aligned}
$$
